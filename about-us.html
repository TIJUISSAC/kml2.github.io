About
Us

KM Industries was founded in 2004 by Shri Jose Kanjamala In Karuvanchal , Kunnar.

After working with his father Shri Joseph Kanjamala for several years, he was inspired to apply his experience and create his won business. For his efforts and success, Mr. Jose Kanjhamal has recived the Best District Entrepreneur Award from the Government of Kerala for the years 2009-2010. 2014 -02015 and again in the year 2017.

Over the years , We have established a reputation of creating growth for all our stake holders, customers, employees and farmers as well. One of the steps we have taken in that direction is to purchase raw material directly from the famrers of Kasaragod, Kozhikode and Kannar.

Our philosophy of “Only Purity” drives us to create products of the highest quality so that our customers can reap all its benefits. To ensure this we monitor the quality of raw material that come into our factories through regular testing. Secondly , we have equipped our factories with state-of-the-art machiney and infrastructure which indludes micro-filtering with a vertical pressure leaf filtering system.
